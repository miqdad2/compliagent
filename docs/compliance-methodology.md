# Compliance Methodology

CompliAgent applies conservative technical review rules:

- Compliance is only marked when direct source-backed evidence satisfies a requirement.
- Missing, indirect, unclear, or non-comparable evidence is marked Ambiguous / Not Proven or Not Verified.
- Numerical comparisons are like-for-like only unless a reliable conversion basis exists.
- Confidence below 70 requires human review.
- Final approval remains with a qualified human reviewer.

Every finding must preserve requirement and evidence source references.
